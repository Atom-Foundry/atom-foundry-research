# The State of AI Recommendations in Supplements

Recommendation Intelligence Research™ · Atom Foundry · June 2026

## Research Summary

We analyzed 4,000 AI-generated supplement recommendations to understand whether AI recommendation frequency is related to AI Commerce Score™.

The answer was no.

Across 400 prompt runs, 371 distinct brands, and thousands of recommendations, recommendation frequency showed virtually no relationship with store readiness.

Correlation between Recommendation Frequency™ and AI Commerce Score™:

r = -0.015

This is statistically indistinguishable from zero.

## Key Findings

- 4,000 recommendations analyzed
- 371 distinct brands identified
- Recommendation Frequency™ and AI Commerce Score™ showed no measurable relationship
- Highly recommended brands frequently had poor AI readiness
- Highly optimized stores often received little recommendation visibility

## Top Recommended Brands

- Garden of Life
- Optimum Nutrition
- Cellucor
- NOW Foods
- Nature Made

## Conclusion

AI systems currently appear to recommend brands they already know rather than stores they can most effectively read and evaluate.

This finding replicates patterns previously observed in Beauty and supports the emerging Recommendation Intelligence™ hypothesis.
